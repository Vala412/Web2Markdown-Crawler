## Export Procedure at PCCCC

### The basic procedure of export at PCCCC includes the following steps

* Filing of shipping bill by CHA/exporter.
* Submission of goods & checklist to BDB (Custodian).
* Registration of goods in customs and allotment of appraiser & examiner to a shipping bill.
* Manual examination order given by appraiser/superintendent.
* As per the examination order, goods examined by the examining officer.
* As per the examination report, assessment done by appraiser/superintendent.
* Assessment approval by DC (Export).
* Packing and submission of goods to BDB.
* Transhipment of goods to Air Cargo Complex.
* Handover of goods at Air Cargo Complex by BDB.

#### The various kinds of shipping bills filed at PCCCC are

* Duty Free Shipping Bills
* Dutiable Shipping Bills (Cess)
* Drawback Shipping Bills
* DEEC Shipping Bills
* EPCG Shipping Bills
* 100% EOU Shipping Bills
* Jobbing Shipping Bills
* Other Exim Scheme Shipping Bills
* NFEI Shipping Bills

The process starting from filing of shipping bills to handover of goods to Air Cargo after LEO are explained in  [PN 14/2017 dated 27/12/2017](Content/images/pdf/Publice-Notice-14-2017.pdf). The PN also gives detailed procedure followed for amendments, Back to town, queries, reprint of Shipping bills and filing of EGM.
