## About us

At the Commissioner of Customs (General), ACC Mumbai Zone-III, we are dedicated to upholding our Citizen’s Charter by providing the trade and industry with efficient, transparent, and timely cargo clearance services. Our team is committed to:

* Ensuring swift and seamless processing of shipments
* Addressing grievances promptly and effectively
* Fostering a culture of service and professionalism among our officers

#### We operate with a strong focus on:

* Integrity and fairness
* Courtesy and empathy
* Transparency and objectivity
* Efficiency and responsiveness

Our goal is to facilitate smooth trade operations while maintaining the highest standards of service and accountability.
