## About us

The Airport Special Cargo Commissionerate (APSC), Mumbai, operates under Customs Zone III, Mumbai, specializing in the regulation and facilitation of high-value and time-sensitive cargo. APSC plays a crucial role in ensuring seamless customs clearance while maintaining strict compliance with national and international trade regulations.

With a focus on efficiency, transparency, and security, APSC oversees the clearance of precious cargo, international courier shipments, and other specialized consignments through its key field formations:

* Precious Cargo Customs Clearance Centre (PCCCC) – Dedicated to handling gold, diamonds, jewelry, and other high-value commodities.
* International Courier Terminal (ICT) – Facilitating express shipments and international courier consignments.
* Airport Sorting Centre (APSO) – Managing the swift processing and clearance of special cargo.

APSC is committed to support India’s trade facilitation initiatives by leveraging technology, enhancing enforcement measures, and ensuring a seamless experience for importers, exporters, and other stakeholders.

### Key Functions of Airport Special Cargo (APSC)

#### Handling Time-Sensitive & High-Value Shipments

* Clearance of urgent consignments like pharmaceuticals, life-saving drugs, and perishable goods.
* Processing courier and express cargo that requires expedited customs procedures.

#### Specialized Cargo Categories

* **Live Animals & Perishables:** Ensuring compliance with quarantine and health regulations.
* **Hazardous Materials:** Enforcing safety and environmental guidelines.
* **Diplomatic & Defense Cargo:** Managing consignments under specific government protocols.

**Airport Special Cargo (APSC) in Mumbai Customs** primarily handles the **customs clearance of specialized cargo** that requires distinct processing due to its nature, urgency, or regulatory requirements. It operates under the Mumbai Air Cargo Complex (ACC) jurisdiction and deals with:

#### Regulatory Compliance & Documentation

* Verifying duty exemptions for government or diplomatic consignments.
* Coordinating with other agencies like the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI), Drug Controller, and Animal Quarantine for clearance approvals.

#### Monitoring & Inspection

* Conducting physical verification and scanning of shipments flagged for examination.
* Implementing risk management systems (RMS) for efficient customs clearance.
