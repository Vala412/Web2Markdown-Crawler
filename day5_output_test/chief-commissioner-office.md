## About Chief Commissioner's Office

[dispalyNone](#)

Chief Commissioner’s Office is headed by the Chief Commissioner who oversees customs and allied Acts related processes of seven Commissionerates viz. Airport, Airport Special Cargo, Import, Export, General, Preventive and Appeals.

This office consists of Additional/Joint Commissioner, Deputy/Assistant Commissioner(s) and supporting officers and staff. Coordination amongst the Commissionerates within the Zone and their effective interaction with the trade to facilitate the import and export operations is handled by the Chief Commissioner’s Office.

The Assistant/Deputy Commissioner, of Chief Commissioner’s Office is designated as CPIO for this office to handle requests under the RTI Act.
