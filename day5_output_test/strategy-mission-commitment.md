### Our Strategy

Our approach is built on a foundation of continuous improvement and innovation, prioritizing efficiency and collaboration. By enhancing the use of Information Technology, we aim to modernize processes and create a seamless, user-friendly experience for all stakeholders. Streamlining customs procedures ensures that operations remain efficient, reduces delays, and enhances transparency.

We emphasize the importance of encouraging voluntary compliance through education and support, fostering trust and cooperation among all participants. Simultaneously, we are committed to evolving cooperative initiatives that bring stakeholders together to achieve shared goals.

Our strategy also includes assisting in the formulation of tariff policies, ensuring they are fair, effective, and reflective of current economic needs. We actively focus on combating fraud effectively, protecting the integrity of our systems and the interests of all stakeholders.

To maintain excellence, we prioritize measuring service delivery standards, continually assessing our performance to identify areas for improvement. Lastly, we are dedicated to developing professionalism and responsibility within our team, fostering a culture of accountability and expertise to ensure the highest standards of service.

### Our Mission is Excellence in Customs Initiatives

Realizing the revenues in a fair, equitable and efficient manner

Administering the Government's economic, tariff and trade policies with a practical approach

Facilitating trade and industry by streamlining and simplifying Customs processes

Creating a climate for voluntary compliance by providing guidance

Combating revenue evasion and commercial frauds effectively
