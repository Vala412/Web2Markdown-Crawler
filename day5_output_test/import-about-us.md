## About us

The **Import Commissionerate of Mumbai Customs Zone III** oversees the smooth clearance of imported goods while ensuring compliance with customs regulations. It manages electronic documentation, duty assessments, refunds, investigations, and enforcement to facilitate trade and safeguard revenue.

#### EDI (Electronic Data Interchange)

* Manages electronic documentation through **Indian Customs EDI System (ICES 1.5)**.
* Operates 24/7 and coordinates with the **Directorate of Systems, New Delhi.**
* Handles software/hardware issues related to EDI operations.

#### Import Groups

* Assesses **Bills of Entry** for clearance under different **Custom Tariff Heads (CTH)**.
* Manages imports for home consumption, warehousing, and bonded goods.

#### Refund

* Processes refunds, including **4% Special Additional Duty (SAD)**.
* Handles refunds under **Section 27 of the Customs Act, 1962**.

#### Import Shed

* Conducts physical examination of imported goods at designated sheds.
* Grants **Out of Charge (OOC)** clearance based on required documentation.
* Processes **Risk Management System (RMS)** Bills of Entry.

#### SIIB (Special Investigation & Intelligence Branch - Import)

* Collects intelligence and conducts investigations on import-related fraud.
* Carries out surveillance, searches, and enforcement actions.

#### MCD (Manifest Clearing Department)

* Maintains **Import & Export General Manifests**.
* Keeps records of **Bills of Entry & Shipping Bills** after closure.

#### Adjudication

* Handles legal adjudication of customs cases.
* Issues notices, schedules hearings, and processes adjudication orders.

#### TRC (Tax Recovery Cell)

* Recovers pending government dues.
* Handles attachment and auction of defaulters' assets.

#### IAD (Internal Audit Department)

* Manages audit objections and compliance tracking.
* Maintains **AM, SOF, and DAP** files.

#### Import Noting

* Maintains **Import General Manifests (IGMs)** for airlines.
* Processes amendments for weight, consignee details, and short shipments.
* Issues **manual Thoka** for SEZ Bills of Entry and consulate cargo.
