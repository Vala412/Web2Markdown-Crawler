## Beware of Fraudsters

* Indian Customs never calls or sends SMS for clearance of goods or customs duty payment.
* Don't fall prey to fraudulent calls, emails, messages and social media posts claiming to be from Indian Customs and demanding payment of customs duty in personal bank accounts.
