Digip is an innovative, Sweden-based online platform designed to streamline the trademark registration process for startups and businesses. We had the opportunity to develop this cutting-edge solution, enabling users to search for trademark availability and complete the registration process online in less than 10 minutes. The platform offers a seamless, efficient, and reliable method for protecting intellectual property, transforming the traditional, time-consuming process into a digital-first experience.

As we embarked on the development of Digip, several challenges arose:

Digip needed a domain purchase and trademark registration flow that could cater to users from multiple linguistic backgrounds.

The platform required a user-friendly design that would allow users to easily navigate the trademark registration process, reducing complexity and ensuring fast completion times.

We needed to integrate third-party APIs to provide real-time domain availability, allowing users to search for and purchase domains directly from the platform.

As Digip's e-commerce functionality expanded, integrating Magento with Next.js became crucial to ensure a smooth and scalable transaction process.

To improve search functionality, we implemented Meilisearch, enabling fast and accurate searches for trademarks and domain availability.

Our development team implemented several technical solutions to meet these challenges:

We built a flexible, multi-language architecture to accommodate users from different regions, ensuring the domain purchase and trademark registration process was fully localized.

We redesigned the user interface with a focus on simplicity and speed. By creating a more intuitive flow, users could complete their trademark registration in under 10 minutes, enhancing user satisfaction and engagement.

We integrated third-party APIs to ensure real-time domain availability and streamlined the process of purchasing relevant domains alongside trademark registration.

The integration of Magento with Next.js enabled better performance and scalability, allowing the platform to handle high volumes of transactions while providing a responsive and dynamic user experience.

By integrating Meilisearch, we significantly improved the platform's search capabilities, allowing users to quickly and efficiently search for trademark availability, enhancing the overall user experience.

By addressing the challenges of multi-language support, UX, and third-party integrations, we helped Digip evolve into a global platform that offers a smooth, efficient, and reliable experience for its users. The use of cutting-edge technologies like Next.js, Magento, and Meilisearch ensured the platform's scalability, responsiveness, and accuracy, setting a new standard for online trademark registration services.
