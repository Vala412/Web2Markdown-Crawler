Introduction
------------

Safety is a leading SaaS platform designed to provide organizations with robust safety and compliance education solutions. With a comprehensive library of over 400 online courses and a user base exceeding one million worldwide, Safety offers a versatile and efficient way to deliver safety training. Trusted by diverse industries including construction, manufacturing, restaurants, and warehouses, Safety ensures seamless employee training, certification, and domain-specific customization for businesses aiming to build their own online training systems.

![Intro Image](/_next/image?url=%2Fimages%2Fportfolio-details%2Fsafety-image2.webp&w=1080&q=75)![Intro Image](/_next/image?url=%2Fimages%2Fportfolio-details%2Fsafety-image3.webp&w=1080&q=75)

The Challenges
--------------

Diverse Course Availability and System Performance

Safety’s vast library of courses necessitated a system capable of handling extensive content without compromising performance. The challenge was to ensure a smooth user experience despite the large volume of courses and training materials available.

Integration of SCORM Courses

Initially, integrating SCORM (Sharable Content Object Reference Model) courses posed a challenge since the platform needed to support these courses without relying on third-party systems. The goal was to implement a seamless and reliable SCORM-compliant system internally.

Comprehensive Tracking and Reporting

Users required an intuitive way to track their training progress, view reports, and manage certifications. Ensuring that the platform maintained accurate and accessible records of training completion and certification status was essential.

Adapting to Classroom Training Needs

Catering to older employees who faced difficulties using digital tools required a different approach. This included providing additional support and making different teams with two managers per team to guide users through the training process.

Database Synchronization and Data Security

Managing and synchronizing databases effectively was crucial. Additionally, ensuring that user data remained secure and was only accessible to authorized individuals was a top priority. Each organization needed a customized experience similar to having their own isolated system, akin to domain registration in Shopify.

Subject Matter Expertise and Commission Model

Incorporating subject matter experts into the platform, similar to Udemy’s concept, presented a new opportunity for Safety to enhance course content and offer expertise. Additionally, implementing a commission model for experts and course creators was a new revenue stream.

Shift from Company-Only Registration to Individual Access

Initially, the platform was designed for company registrations only. Expanding access to individuals who could register, select courses, and receive certifications required significant system adjustments.

The Solution
------------

Enhanced System Performance

Safety upgraded its infrastructure to handle a vast array of courses efficiently, ensuring smooth navigation and quick access to training materials without performance degradation.

In-House SCORM Integration

The team developed a robust SCORM-compliant module that allowed seamless integration of SCORM courses without relying on external systems. This internal solution provided greater control and reliability.

Support for Classroom Training

Recognizing the need for additional support, Safety established dedicated teams and managers to assist older employees with digital tools and email communication. This support helped bridge the gap and improve the user experience.

Database Synchronization and Data Security

Safety implemented a sophisticated database synchronization mechanism and robust security protocols. Each organization had a customized experience with isolated data access, ensuring privacy and security.

Subject Matter Expert Module

The platform introduced a subject matter expert module, allowing industry experts to contribute to course content. This module also implemented a commission model, creating new revenue opportunities for both experts and Safety.

Expanded Access and Individual Registration

Safety adapted its system to allow individual users to register, access the course library, and receive certifications. This expansion increased the platform’s reach and accessibility.

The Impact
----------

The implementation of these solutions led to several significant improvements for Safety:

Streamlined Installation and Setup

The new system eliminated the need for manual software installation and setup, reducing installation charges to zero. This made it easier for both companies and individual users to get started with the platform.

Increased Revenue

The introduction of the subject matter expert module and individual registration contributed to increased income. The commission model incentivized experts to create high-quality content, further enhancing the platform’s offerings.

Enhanced User Experience

The improved system performance, comprehensive tracking, and reporting features provided a more efficient and user-friendly experience. Support for older employees and individual access made the platform more inclusive and accessible.

Secure and Customized Experience

Database synchronization and security measures ensured that user data was protected and each organization had a tailored experience, similar to having their own isolated system.

In conclusion, Safety’s innovative solutions addressed a range of challenges, from handling diverse course content to implementing secure and customized experiences. By enhancing system performance, integrating SCORM courses, and expanding access to individual users, Safety has established itself as a premier platform for online safety training. The successful implementation of these solutions has not only improved the user experience but also contributed to increased revenue and growth opportunities for the platform.
